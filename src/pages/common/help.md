## Account Setup <a name="account"></a>

### How do I Register for a free trial? <a name="register"></a>

Access the Register link via the many buttons saying "Free Signup" on home page menu, and fill in your details on the form.
<!-- ![register-form](images/help/registerform.webp) -->
Don't forget to accept the license and Terms of Service!
<!-- ![register-form2](images/help/registerform2.webp) -->
Once successfully registered you will be presented with the login form.
<!-- ![register-form3](images/help/login.webp) -->

___

### How do I Login in to use the web app? <a name="login"></a>

Access the login link via the home page menu, and fill in your login details on the form.
<!-- ![login-form](images/help/loginform.webp) -->
___

### I forgot my password. What do I do? <a name="reset"></a>

Press the Forgot Password button on the login form (see above), and fill in your email to receive a link via email to create a new password. 
<!-- ![reset-form](images/help/resetform.webp) -->

You can also reset your password any time from the profile section of the app. 
___ 

### Account Verification <a name="verify"></a>

You will need to verify your email account to access the free account credits and use the store to subscribe or buy tokens.
Click on the verify button in your account profile section to receive a verification email with link.
<!-- ![login-form](images/help/verify.webp) -->
Click on the link in the verification email and you will be redirected to the LabelLens.com app and your account verified.
<!-- ![login-form](images/help/verified.webp) -->
You will also be assigned your free credits & save limits.
<!-- ![login-form](images/help/verifiedcredits.webp) -->

### Which Browser should I use? <a name="usebrowser"></a>

For the most reliable experience it is recommended to use Chrome browser on all platforms, desktop or mobile. 
Next best choice would be Firefox. 
Please don't forget to authorise LabelLens for access to the camera when requested.
You can also check the browser site settings/privacy and security/camera settings anytime. You can read more about how to do that here: https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/2693767
